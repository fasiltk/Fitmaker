Kidney Patients Exercise Guidelines

Type of Exercise Can Kidney Patients Do? Cannot Do
Low-impact Cardiovascular Exercise Walking, cycling, swimming High-impact activities like running or jumping
Strength Training Light resistance training with proper form and supervision Heavy lifting or intense strength training without medical approval
Flexibility Exercises Gentle stretching and yoga Overstretching or intense flexibility exercises
Aerobic Exercises Moderate-intensity aerobic exercises High-intensity exercises without medical approval
Breathing Exercises Controlled breathing exercises Strenuous breath-holding exercises or extreme deep breathing
Balance Exercises Simple balance exercises with support Complex or challenging balance exercises without supervision
Water Exercises Swimming or water aerobics in consultation with a healthcare provider Prolonged exposure to extremely hot or cold water
Anaerobic Exercises Short bursts of activity with rest periods Prolonged anaerobic exercises without medical guidance
Sports Activities Low-intensity sports like golf or bowling Contact sports or activities with a high risk of injury
