Exercise Guidelines for Disc Patients

Type of Exercise Can Do Cannot Do
Low-Impact Aerobic Exercise Walking, swimming, stationary cycling High-impact activities (running, jumping)
Core Strengthening Exercises Pelvic tilts, gentle abdominal exercises Sit-ups, double leg raises
Flexibility and Stretching Gentle stretching of the spine and hamstrings Excessive or forceful twisting
Strength Training Light weights, focusing on good form Heavy lifting, improper form
Yoga and Pilates Modified poses with proper alignment Deep forward bends, full twists
Cardiovascular Exercise Low-impact activities for cardiovascular health Prolonged high-impact activities
