Back Bone Exercise Guidelines

Type of Exercise Can Do Should Avoid
Aerobic Exercise - Walking
- Swimming
- Cycling		 - High-impact activities (running, jumping)
- Prolonged sitting
- Excessive bending or twisting
- Jerky, high-impact movements
Strength Training - Core-stabilizing exercises (planks, bridges)
- Low-resistance, high-repetition exercises
- Leg exercises (with proper form and low weights)		 - Heavy lifting without proper form
- Deadlifts without proper form
- Overhead presses without support
- Twisting with added weights
Flexibility - Stretching (gentle, controlled movements)
- Yoga (moderate and modified poses)		 - Overstretching or bouncing during stretches
- Deep forward bends or twists without modifications
Cardiovascular - Elliptical trainer
- Stationary bike		 - High-impact aerobics
- Trampoline exercises
- Sudden, vigorous movements
Low-Impact Sports - Golf
- Bowling		 - High-impact sports (football, basketball)
- Activities with a high risk of falling
- Extreme sports (skiing, snowboarding)
