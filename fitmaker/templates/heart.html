Exercise Guidelines for Heart Patients

Type of Exercise Recommended for Heart Patients? Intensity Duration Frequency Notes
Aerobic (Cardiovascular) Yes Moderate to Vigorous 150 minutes per week (gradually build up) 3-5 times per week Examples: Walking, cycling, swimming. Monitor heart rate and stay within target heart rate zone recommended by the doctor.
Strength Training Yes Light to Moderate 2-3 sessions per week Allow at least 48 hours between sessions Use proper form, start with light weights, and progress gradually.
Flexibility and Stretching Yes Gentle stretching 5-10 minutes per session 2-3 times per week Helps improve flexibility and reduce the risk of injury.
High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) Consult with a healthcare provider Varies based on individual capacity Short bursts of intense activity alternated with rest or low-intensity periods Individualized based on health status Not recommended for everyone; should be done under supervision.
Isometric Exercises Consult with a healthcare provider Light to Moderate Brief, controlled muscle contractions Individualized based on health status Avoid breath-holding during isometric exercises. Consult with a healthcare provider before starting.
